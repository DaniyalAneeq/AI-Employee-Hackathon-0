## Draft Post

We just shipped something we're genuinely proud of — our **Gold Tier AI Employee** is live. 🤖

For the past few weeks we've been quietly building an autonomous Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) that runs 24/7 and handles real business operations — not demos, not simulations.

Here's what it does today:

📧 **Monitors Gmail** — triages incoming emails, drafts replies, and flags anything needing human attention
📊 **Manages Accounting in Odoo** — creates invoices, tracks outstanding payments, and generates financial summaries automatically
📱 **Handles Social Media** — drafts and schedules posts on Facebook and Instagram, then waits for your approval before publishing
🔐 **Human-in-the-Loop on everything sensitive** — no payment, no post, no email goes out without you approving it first

The key insight that made this work: instead of a chatbot that waits for you to type, this system uses lightweight Python "Watcher" scripts that monitor your inboxes and trigger the AI automatically. Claude Code acts as the reasoning engine, Obsidian as the memory, and MCP servers as the hands.

We built this for the GIAIC Hackathon 0 — and it's entirely local-first. Your data stays on your machine.

If you're a business owner tired of repetitive admin work eating your day — this is what the next generation of AI tooling looks like.
